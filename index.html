Traffic Simulator

Mathematical traffic-flow models — micro and macro — interactive and configurable. Built in the browser with vector graphics and real physical units.

Macroscopic Models — continuum PDEs

Macro 1st Order

LWR — Kinematic Wave

Lighthill–Whitham–Richards model. Density evolves as a scalar conservation law with Greenshields equilibrium. Includes travel-time heatmap and passive micro-car visualisation.

∂ρ/∂t + ∂(ρ·v_e(ρ))/∂x = 0
Macro 2nd Order

ARZ — Aw–Rascle–Zhang

Second-order model with driver anticipation. Avoids the wrong-way traffic instability of Payne–Whitham. Rusanov scheme with operator-split relaxation.

∂ρ/∂t + ∂(ρv)/∂x = 0
∂y/∂t + ∂(yv)/∂x = ρ(v_e−v)/τ

Microscopic Models — individual vehicles

Micro

OVM / IDM — Car-following

Optimal Velocity Model and Intelligent Driver Model. Individual vehicles react to headway and relative speed. Live real-time ring-road simulation.
